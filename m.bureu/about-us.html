S E T L A is a studio for architectural visualization. The team of architects tries to solve every challenge, with its diversity and different views through the implementation of innovative knowledge.

We find inspiration in nature and natural sensations.

The creativity of our team is based on precise reflection, imitation of materials and everything that surrounds us. The fascination of games between light and shadow, unique phenomena, make us find the satisfaction in details. In that way, we create an impression that our work is photorealistic.

Our mission is focused on the very simple principle, to work together with a client as an equal partner. We cooperate in close partnership with architects, designers, marketing agencies and in a proactive way we give a new form of vision, approaching it to reality. The finish line is to provide innovative and reliable solutions that improve the business competitiveness of clients. We believe in building a long-term business cooperation between our clients and us. Very seriously, we understand and respect the time of our clients.

S E T L A Studio specializes in the following areas:
  • Exterior Visualization
  • Interior Visualization
  • Architectural Modeling
  • VR 360 Reality
  • Photomontage of 3D and Photography
  • High-Poly Furniture Modeling
